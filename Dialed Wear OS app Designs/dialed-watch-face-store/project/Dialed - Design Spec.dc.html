Dialed — Design Spec · v1

Dark showroom, premium horology.

A boutique display case for Wear OS watch faces. The canvas is near-black so the faces provide every drop of color; one champagne-gold accent carries actions, connection, and unlock moments. Everything below maps 1:1 to Jetpack Compose + Material 3 — exact tokens and motion specs live in the handoff section and in HANDOFF.md.

Assumptions
  • 8 placeholder faces, rendered live — swap with real face art later
  • Price $11.99 shown as placeholder
  • Reference device: Pixel 9 phone + Pixel Watch 4
  • Type: Instrument Sans (Google Fonts → Compose downloadable fonts)

B · Brand & logo

Three circular marks

All built on a perfect circle, all monochrome-safe. Recommendation: 1c — it animates naturally (the hand sweeps to 1 o'clock on launch) and survives tiny sizes. 1b is the strongest letterform if you want the D to be ownable.

1aIndex — ring + gold marker at 12

A bezel with one lume index. Calm, horological, zero ambiguity at 16dp. Least distinctive of the three.

1bAperture D — stem + bowl, gold gap at 1

The D as a dial: vertical stem, circular bowl, and a gold marker sitting in the gap at 1 o'clock. Most ownable letterform.

1cSweep — dial + single gold hand · recommended

A minimal dial, hand at 1 o'clock — "dialed in". On launch the hand sweeps from 12 and settles with a spring. Works from 512dp to 16dp.

Adaptive app icon (built on 1c) — dark · light · monochrome · themed

Foreground = mark at 60% of safe zone; background = flat #0B0B0D (dark) / #F2EFE8 (light). Monochrome layer for Android 13+ themed icons.
